About Us

After Midnight Tattoos is a Johannesburg-based studio led by Marcel Geyer, specializing in flash, stencils, and custom work. Open daily from 12:00 PM – 12:00 AM.

Meet the Artist

Marcel Geyer blends traditional flash with modern stencil precision. His work spans script, blackwork, and geometric styles.
